Quality: About This Data Set

The data was retrieved from official websites. However, there are some quality issues in the electricity demand report. There is not information regarding electricity demand for six departments, reason why I didn't include them on the data set.

Is the data complete?

The data is incomplete. There is not information regarding energy consumption for six departments. It is not clear if the data is missing, or if those departments do not have electricity demand report because are not connected to the central grid. What is your evidence for this?

Is the data coherent?

The data make sense. I think that it is useful to compare energy demand and energy access to know if departments with a high energy consumption are concentrating their consumption in urban areas and living aside the development of rural areas

Is the data correct?

The information about the Colombian energy sector is limited, so, it is difficult to verify if it is correct. However, the sources are official, so we can trust that is the best information that is available
